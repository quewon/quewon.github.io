The world is big. I work small. Like the restrained poetry of a song, in games I like simple interactions within contained spaces. I have a bit of an obsession with public transit systems and Tetris. Lately I've been playing chess.

I keep my materials basic. I usually script in plain HTML and javascript. Sometimes I use light and specialized game tools like Pico-8, Twine, or Bitsy. I like the web, how it's easily shared and accessed by a variety of people on a variety of devices.

When I get bored of flat digital worlds, I play with 3d printing or knitting.
