[R-TYPE]([https://github.com/quewon/r-type](https://github.com/quewon/r-type)) (meaning receipt typewriter) is a primitive terminal writing program that live prints to a mini receipt printer that uses 58mm thermal paper, connected via USB.

![Screenshot of the program in action.](/static/attachments/text/receipt.png)

![A printed receipt.](/static/attachments/text/receipt_printed.png)

The writing experience is limiting. Only 32 characters can fit on a line, and once you run over or break a line, you cannot edit the previous line. You can format your text bold and double-wide, as is supported by the printer.
